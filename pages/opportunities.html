Grace Hopper Conference 2019

The Grace Hopper Conference is the world’s largest and best technology conference that celebrates women in
technology. GHC is an incredible celebration filled with women in technology from all over the world. The last
celebration had over 18,000 attendees from 81 different countries with over 400 sessions and 800 speakers. It’s a welcoming place to network, learn, and find community.

It will take place between October 2 to 4, 2019 in Orlando, Florida. Scholarships opened on January 16, 2019 at 9am P.T and will close on March 6, 2019. General registerations will open mid-July.
